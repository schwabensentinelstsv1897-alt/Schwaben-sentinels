## Getting Started in 2 Minutes

### Step 1 — Open the link
Works on any phone or computer — no download needed.
On iPhone: tap Share → **Add to Home Screen** for quick access like a real app.

### Step 2 — Select your name & log in
- Select your name from the dropdown
- Enter the **temporary PIN** the admin will share with you
- Set your own **4-digit PIN** — you'll use this every time
- Done ✅

---

## What You Need to Do

### ✅ Mark your availability — every match
Go to **50 Overs** or **T20** tab and tap **Available / Maybe / Unavailable** for each match.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Availability locks **16 days before** the match. Mark early!

### 🏋 Practice sessions
Check the **Practice** tab and respond to upcoming sessions.

### 🏏 Friendly matches
Check the **Friendly** tab when friendlies are added.

## Your PIN is Personal
- Do not share your PIN with anyone
- You can change it anytime: Team tab → your name → More details → 🔐 Change PIN
- If you forget it, ask the admin to reset it
